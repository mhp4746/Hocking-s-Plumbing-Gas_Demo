Rather not do it yourself?

Getting a professional in saves you a lot of work — and often a lot of money. You'd buy a pile of tools you'll never use again, and if you're not experienced, doing it yourself costs hours of wasted time.

A common mistake is planning a new layout around the old plumbing so you don't have to move it — and ending up with a room that cost thousands but is just as impractical as before.

Fixing mistakes costs even more

The biggest reason to get a pro in? You won't pay someone to fix your mistakes later — usually far more costly than doing it right the first time.

Picture breaking out brand-new tiles to fix a botched shower waterproofing job. From ordering the right parts to staying compliant with plumbing regulations (many there for your health and safety), we've got the experience, tools and equipment to nail it.

How your reno works with us

Clear plumbing, start to finish.

No guesswork and no surprises — here's exactly how we handle the plumbing side of your bathroom, kitchen or laundry.

  1. 01

    Free on-site quote

    We come out, look at your space and your plans, then give you a clear, fixed price up front — obligation-free.

  2. 02

    Plan the layout

    We work out where the water, drains and gas need to go so your new layout works — instead of squeezing it around old pipes.

  3. 03

    Rough-in

    We relocate the pipework and drains and leave it ready for your tiler and cabinetmaker, on a schedule that suits the other trades.

  4. 04

    Fit-off & compliance

    We install the tapware, vanity, shower and toilet, test it all, and hand you a signed Certificate of Compliance — backed by our 7-year warranty.
