We'll clear your blocked drains.

Blocked drains are unpleasant, unhygienic and inconvenient — bringing your home or business to a standstill. When the blockage just won't budge, give us a call.

Fast, thorough, lasting

Fixed at the source

One of the biggest reasons we're called out is to help with blocked drains — from toilets to showers and sinks. We don't just clear the symptom; we find what's actually causing the blockage so it stays gone.

There are a lot of reasons drains become blocked. Some you can control — others, not so much.

Photo goes here assets/service-drains.jpg

Things you can control

Keep these out of your drains.

Hair

The most common cause. Clear hair from sink, shower and bath traps regularly to stop it clogging up.

Plants & roots

Tree roots and leaves back up pipes fast. Keep gutters clear and avoid root-hungry palms, willows and gums.

Grease

Kitchen grease cools and hardens in the pipe. Never pour it down the sink — bin it instead.

Toiletries

Wipes, nappies and sanitary items — even "flushable" ones — swell and catch. Bin them, don't flush them.

Foreign objects

Soap, food scraps and anything dropped down the drain will cause a blockage sooner or later. So just don't.

Things you can't control

And the ones we'll handle for you.

Heavy rain

You can't stop the rain — but keeping gutters clean and clear of leaves goes a long way.

Bad installation

Poor plumbing damages water flow and invites blockages. Renovating? Get a plumber on board early.

Poor water flow

Sediment builds up inside pipes and slows everything down. We'll clear it and get the flow back.
