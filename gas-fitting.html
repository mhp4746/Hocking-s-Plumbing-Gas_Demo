Don't risk it — call the professionals.

Working with gas is dangerous and poses a threat to you, your family and your property. Performing gas work without a licence is illegal — so make sure it's done correctly and safely.

Fully licensed gas work

Taking care of all the gas work

From a new cooktop to a full appliance install, our licensed gas fitters do it safely and sign it off properly. Here's what we take care of:

  • Install and repair gas pipes, bayonet points and connections
  • Test pipework for gas appliances
  • Install gas appliances — cooktops, wall ovens, upright cookers and hot water systems
  • Install gas appliance flues
  • Install gas detection and pressure-regulating systems

Smell gas? This is an emergency.

If you suspect a leak or smell gas, turn off the gas at the mains, open windows and doors, avoid any naked flame or electrical switches — and call us immediately on 0439 906 806. We're on call day and night.
