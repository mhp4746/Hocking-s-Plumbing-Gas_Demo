Our story

From apprentice to owner

Hockings Plumbing & Gas began as an apprenticeship with my father back in 2007. I completed my trade certificate and gas fitting licence in 2010, then spent more than five years working as a plumber, tradesman and manager.

I wanted to build a business around what customers actually want — fair quotes, reliability, and doing exactly what we say we will. So in July 2015, after completing my Plumbers Contractors Licence, I took over Hockings Plumbing & Gas.

It's proven a sound strategy: strong customer loyalty and plenty of word-of-mouth referrals. As we've grown, we've added two apprentices to the team — and we're still going.

— Jared Hocking, Owner
