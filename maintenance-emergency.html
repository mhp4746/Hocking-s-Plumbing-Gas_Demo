Know the signs

When should you call an emergency plumber?

A dripping tap or leaking toilet can wait. But some situations need a plumber straight away — for your safety, or to stop the damage to your property.

No hot water

Getting through a day without hot water is a struggle. If the power's fine and only the system's out, give us a call.

Burst pipes

A burst pipe can ruin walls, floors and belongings fast. Turn off the water at the mains, then call us to limit the damage.

Blocked toilets & drains

If a toilet's backing up or sewage appears where it shouldn't, call straight away — it gets costly very quickly.

Water leak

A small leak becomes a big bill fast. Turn off the mains tap, then call us before it wastes water and damages your home.

Gas leak

Extremely dangerous. Turn off the mains valve, open windows, avoid flames and switches — and call a gas fitter immediately.

Prevention beats repair

Plumbing maintenance

Maintenance is the best way to lower the risk of plumbing and gas emergencies — and it helps your appliances, fittings and fixtures last longer.

We can help you maintain your heating units, clear your sink, shower and bath wastes, and unclog toilets. We even look after your gutters — repairs, maintenance and installation, including rainwater tanks.
