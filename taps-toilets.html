Repairs & replacements

Leaking or dripping taps

It may seem simple, but there are a few reasons a tap drips. If it drips while it's turned off, it's probably the washer. If water escapes from the body of the tap or under the cover plate when it's on, it's most likely the O-ring that needs replacing.

Either way, it's tools-out, hands-dirty work — so while it's apart, it's worth replacing both. Or you can get us to take care of it for you.

Toilet & cistern repairs

More often than not, a leaking toilet comes down to a washer needing replacement — a fairly quick and easy job. Sometimes it's a little more involved, like replacing the cistern outlet kit.

Rather avoid the hassle of pulling it apart, matching parts at the hardware store and hoping it fits? Give us a call and it's sorted.

Whitegoods & fridge connections

Get a whitegoods installation wrong and you'll wish you'd had it done properly. Done right, we avoid leaks into the dishwasher and dirty-water backflow from poor drainage.

We also handle fridge water connections — tapping into an existing pipe with the correct fixtures and fittings, so it lasts and stays compliant with regulations.
